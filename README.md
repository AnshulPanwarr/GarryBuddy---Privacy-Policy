Privacy Policy for GariBuddy

Last Updated: May 2025

GariBuddy ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the GariBuddy mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use GariBuddy, we collect the following information:

Google Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture when you sign in with Google.
Car Information: Vehicle details you provide such as brand, model, year, fuel type, registration number, and odometer readings.
Service Records: Maintenance logs, fuel fill-ups, expenses, insurance details, and reminders you create.
Usage Data: App interaction patterns to improve user experience.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Provide and maintain the GariBuddy service
Store and sync your car records across devices
Send you service reminders and notifications
Improve app features and user experience
We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information. Your car records are associated with your Google account and are only accessible to you.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

When required by law or legal process
To protect the rights and safety of GariBuddy and its users
With your explicit consent
5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access your personal data stored in the app
Delete your account and all associated data
Export your car records
Opt out of non-essential notifications
6. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your GariBuddy account at any time from within the app:

Open GariBuddy and go to your Profile
Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
Confirm the deletion in the dialog
When you delete your account, the following data is permanently and irreversibly removed from our servers:

Your Google account information (name, email, profile picture)
All vehicle records (cars, details, odometer readings)
All service records, fuel logs, expenses, and reminders
Your Firebase authentication credentials
This action cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Deletion is processed immediately upon confirmation.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all data is permanently removed from our Firebase servers immediately. We do not retain backups of deleted user data.

8. Children's Privacy

GariBuddy is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
